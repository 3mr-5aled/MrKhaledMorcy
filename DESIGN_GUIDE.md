## ✨ **Typography Guidelines**

### **Arabic Font:**

- **Primary**: Cairo (Bold, SemiBold for headings)
- **Alternative**: Tajawal, Almarai
- **Characteristics**: Modern, clean, highly legible

### **English Font:**

- **Primary**: Inter, Poppins, Montserrat
- **Style**: Sans-serif, modern, professional
- **Weight**: 600-700 for headings, 400-500 for body

### **Text Hierarchy:**

- **Main Name**: 48-72px (Bold)
- **Subtitle/Profession**: 24-32px (SemiBold)
- **Body/Details**: 16-20px (Regular)

## 💡 **Additional Tips**

1. **Consistency**: Use the same profile picture across all platforms
2. **Recognition**: Keep branding elements consistent (colors, style, layout)
3. **Clarity**: Ensure text is readable at thumbnail sizes
4. **Cultural**: Respect Arabic RTL layout and typography
5. **Professional**: Avoid overly casual or playful designs
6. **Updates**: Refresh banners seasonally or for special announcements
7. **Testing**: Preview designs at actual display sizes before finalizing

---

## 📁 **File Formats**

- **Profile Pictures**: PNG with transparency (1080x1080px)
- **Banners**: JPG or PNG (1500x500px or platform-specific)
- **Color Mode**: RGB (for digital display)
- **Resolution**: 72-150 DPI for web, 300 DPI for print
